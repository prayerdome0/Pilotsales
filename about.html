About Pilot Sales Enterprise

We connect talented individuals with commission-based job opportunities. Our mission is to help people earn income remotely, with flexibility and transparency.

Who We Are

Your Partner in Remote Earnings

Pilot Sales Enterprise was founded with a simple mission: to create legitimate, commission-based work opportunities for people around the world. We believe that anyone with dedication and a smartphone can earn income.

We currently offer two main job positions: Home Loan Outreach Representative and Website Seller. Both roles are 100% remote, flexible, and pay weekly commissions in cryptocurrency.

Our team is based in the USA and Philippines, serving members from over 20 countries including Philippines, USA, Zambia, Nigeria, Kenya, and more.

Our Mission

To provide legitimate, transparent, and fair commission-based job opportunities that empower individuals to earn income remotely, regardless of their location or experience level.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted platform for remote commission-based work, connecting thousands of workers with businesses that need their help, while maintaining integrity and fairness.
